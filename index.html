Inspire Me

Feeling uninspired? Down in the dumps? Life seeming a little lackluster? We’ve all been there..

Now when you’re feeling low you can open up Find My Inspiration! With just the click of a button, Find My Inspiration will randomly generate two inspirational quotes to help you feel better or give you some insight into life. After reading both, you can then vote on which one lifted your spirits more, which will help others who are also in a rough spot in life.
